More about me CV ↓

I started with quantum measurement theory and cavity optomechanics at Moscow State University, where I did my BSc and MSc at once. From there I went to EPFL for integrated photonics and squeezed light, then spent a year at KAUST on photonic neural networks and quantum-dot lasers. I'm now a Research Assistant in the Photonics Lab at ETH Zurich.

I prefer to work across fields rather than within one, pulled along mostly by curiosity and a stubborn need to understand how things actually work.
